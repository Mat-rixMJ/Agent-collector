# Competitor Analysis: The Trading Channel (TTC University)

## Positioning
TTC University is positioned as an educational platform offering a focused learning path on the Forex Channel method, with some free content available via YouTube.

## Target Audience
Beginners in forex trading who are interested in structured learning and practical simulations to build their skills.

## Pricing Model
One-time fee (varies), with core material also available for free on YouTube.

## Dominant Content Format/Channel
Video-only lessons primarily distributed through YouTube.
